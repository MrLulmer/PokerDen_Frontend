1. Introduction

This website, developed as a final-year gymnasium thesis project at Freie Schule Zurich, aims to assist amateur poker players in improving their skills. It is designed as a learning tool, not to promote gambling, but to enhance the understanding and appeal of poker.

Also, a large part of the project was to gain an insight in frontend developing and programming as a hole.

The programmes used where:

• VS-Code; as a code editor

• GitHub; as a node to store and share the code

• Figma; to develop graphics

• Netlify; to secure a domain

• HotJar; as a tracker
